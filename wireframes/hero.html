Design intent

  • LayoutTwo-column split. ~55 / 45. Text left, illustration right. Full viewport height. Break to single column below 900px.
  • MotionH1 types out word by word. Stats count up when in view. Illustration parallax on mouse move. Scroll cue bobs gently.
  • BackgroundSolid or subtle gradient. No Three.js mountain (competes with illustration).
  • KickerPill-shaped badge, top-left. Quiet, not shouty.
  • H1Large display type. Three lines, breaks intentionally set. Each phrase lands on its own line.
  • Stats barInline with the text column, not a separate strip. Four numbers grouped tightly. Count up once.
  • Scroll cuePill-shaped button, not floating text. Clicking smooth-scrolls to Chapter 2.
  • Terminal widgetRemoved from hero. Moves to Chapter 6 where it belongs (I Direct, They Execute).
