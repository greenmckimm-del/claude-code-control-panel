07

"When the Conversation Ends, the Knowledge Stays"

AI conversations are ephemeral. When the chat window closes, the context disappears. I solved this with a second brain. Every significant session logs to my Obsidian vault. Every architectural decision is saved to memory files. Every project has a living backlog. When Claude starts a new session, across any IDE or device, it picks up exactly where it left off.
